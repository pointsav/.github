# PointSav Digital Systems
### *Trustworthy Records Infrastructure* · *Infraestructura de Registros Verificables*

</div>

<br/>

We build operating systems for institutions that need permanent, portable, and verifiable records tied to specific legal assets. Each archive is anchored to a land title, a company registration, or a person — and belongs entirely to that asset. The records are stored as universal flat files. The security foundation is formally verified. When an asset changes hands, the complete history transfers with it.

Woodfine Management Corp., a real property management company developing environmentally certified commercial properties across North America and Europe, is our first institutional deployment. Their complete operational fleet is publicly documented and in active development.

The Sovereign Data Foundation in Denmark is the intended equity holder and planned overseer of open-source integrity. This arrangement has not yet been formally executed.
