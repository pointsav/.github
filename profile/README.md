### 🎯 The Institutional Mandate
PointSav Digital Systems engineers independent operating infrastructure for the modern enterprise. In an era of increasing cyber risk and regulatory scrutiny, relying on shared legacy SaaS vulnerabilities (SaaS) databases is a structural liability. 

We build decentralized, privately-owned networks that allow institutional stewards to hold their operational data as secure, physical assets. By deploying native architectures atop formally verified microkernels, we structurally bypass the vulnerabilities inherent in commercial hyperscaler infrastructure.

### ⚖️ Verifiable Data Ownership
Compliance must be an automated mathematical reality, not a manual corporate policy. Our architecture natively enforces strict data segregation, guaranteeing SOC 3 (Processing Integrity) and DARP compliance by design. We eliminate vendor lock-in, ensuring enterprises maintain absolute, unencumbered custodial control over their digital history.
